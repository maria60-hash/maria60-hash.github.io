Featured Recipe: Pineapple Fried Rice

A sweet and savory favorite—great for brunch!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups day-old cooked rice
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup peas and carrots
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp shoyu (soy sauce)

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs in a pan and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry rice; add veggies and pineapple.
  3. Stir in shoyu and fold in eggs. Serve hot.

Conclusion

This pineapple fried rice brings a taste of Hawaiʻi straight to your kitchen. With its perfect balance of sweet pineapple and savory rice, it captures the spirit of the islands in every bite. The recipe is simple enough for beginners but delicious enough to impress your friends and family. Whether you’re cooking for brunch, lunch, or dinner, this dish adds a bright, tropical touch to the table. Serve it warm, enjoy the colors and aromas, and share the aloha with everyone around you — because great food, like good company, is always better when it’s shared.
